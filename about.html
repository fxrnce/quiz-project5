What is Webwiz: Tag It & Style It!?

Our mission is to make learning HTML and CSS fun, interactive, and effective for students specially who study Web Application and Development. Our quiz platform tests your knowledge and tracks your progress as we cover the topics from basic HTML tags to advanced CSS styling. It is designed to challenge your understanding and help you improve step by step.

One question at a time. Start your journey, level up your skills, and become a web wizard!
